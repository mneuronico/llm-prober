How does sourdough starter actually work?
assistant
Sourdough starter is a naturally occurring mixture of wild yeast and bacteria that plays a crucial role in the fermentation process of sourdough bread. It's a fascinating microorganism that breaks down the sugars in the dough, producing lactic acid, carbon dioxide, and other compounds that contribute to the characteristic flavor and texture
