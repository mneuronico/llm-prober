Topic:
Activation steering and causal interventions on internal representations

Why this matters to the paper:
These papers motivate the causal side of your argument. Prior steering work mainly uses internal interventions to change behavior, style, refusal, or truthfulness, whereas your paper uses steering as a test of whether self-report depends on probe-defined state and whether introspection quality itself can be improved. This subsection is therefore where you connect intervention methods to causal validation of your operational introspection measure.
