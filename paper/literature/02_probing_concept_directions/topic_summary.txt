Topic:
Probing internal representations and behavioral concept directions

Why this matters to the paper:
This folder supports your measurement apparatus. These papers justify the use of linear probes and concept directions while also making clear that probe scores alone do not settle what a model "really" represents. For your paper, the right framing is that probes are operational tools that need validation, and that self-report plus steering provide convergent evidence for whether a direction is scientifically meaningful.
