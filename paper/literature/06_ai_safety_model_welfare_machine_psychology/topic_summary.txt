Topic:
AI safety, model welfare, and machine psychology

Why this matters to the paper:
This bucket gives the broader stakes for why introspective self-report matters without overclaiming that the paper measures consciousness, suffering, or genuine subjective experience. These works support the position that self-reports may matter ethically or for safety, but that they need validation before they can be trusted. In your paper, this section should stay bounded: the contribution is a methodological tool for evaluating whether self-reports are informative, not a resolution of welfare or consciousness debates.
