Topic:
Temporal dynamics and conversation-induced state drift in LLMs

Why this matters to the paper:
This subsection grounds the "through time" dimension of your experiments. The papers here show that model behavior, persona, instruction following, and confidence can drift across turns, which supports your claim that introspection should be measured turn by turn rather than only in pooled or single-shot settings. In your paper, this literature motivates per-turn analysis and the need to separate genuine probe-report coupling from correlations driven only by conversation time.
