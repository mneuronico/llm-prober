Topic:
Introspection and self-report in language models

Why this matters to the paper:
This is the core related-work bucket for the project. It establishes that LLM self-report, self-evaluation, and metacognition are active research areas, but most prior work stays close to correctness, confidence, or benchmark-style self-assessment. Relative to that literature, the paper's main distinction is the operational test of whether numeric self-reports track probe-defined internal state in natural multi-turn conversations, from early turns onward, and whether that coupling can be shown to change over time and respond to interventions.
